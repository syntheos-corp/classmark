MEMORANDUM FOR THE UNDER SECRETARY OF DEFENSE FOR POLICY

FROM: Director, Net Assessment

SUBJECT: Strategic Assessment of Soviet Capabilities

DATE: 15 March 1978

1. (C) This memorandum provides an assessment of Soviet strategic
capabilities as of 1 January 1978.

2. (C) Intelligence indicates continued expansion of ICBM forces
with deployment of new SS-18 and SS-19 missiles.

3. (U) Public statements by Soviet leadership continue to emphasize
peaceful coexistence while military programs suggest otherwise.

4. (C) Recommended that DOD monitor Soviet SALT compliance closely
and prepare alternative force postures.
